About Me

I'm an Embedded Engineer at SanDisk Device Design Centre (Brisa-technologies), where I focus on developing and optimizing embedded systems. My passion lies in bridging the gap between hardware and software to create efficient, reliable solutions. As part of the ASIC PCIe validation team, I blend automation with meticulous detective work. I use tools like Jenkins for systematic testing, JTAG and Metaware Debugger for deep firmware inspection, Also PCIe Protocol Analyzers to capture traces and JTAG and PCIe protocol analyzers to decipher complex hardware states and guarantee robustness.
